# Our Cookbook
## Pancakes
- 195g Flour
- 2 tbsp Sugar
- 1 tbsp baking powder
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 295ml milk
- 1 large egg
- 70g Butter
## Carbonara
- 100g Spaghetti
- 3 Eggs
- 100g Guanciale
- 100g Pecorino Romano
- Pasta Water
- Pepper
## Omelette
- 3 eggs
- 50g Gruyere
- 15ml milk
- Salt & Pepper to taste
- 1 tsp Butter
- 1 Onion
## Tiramisu
- 250g Mascarpone
- 3 Eggs
- 200g Ladyfingers
- 2 cups Espresso
- Cocoa powder for dusting
